About Me

Hello! I'm Paulo Jonathan P. Lee, a rookie developer based in Quezon City. I have a skill for creating web applications. I started my journey in web development during my college years at FEU Institute of Technology, where I majored in Animation and Game Development.

Over the past few months, I have developed a skill set on HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I enjoy problem-solving and seek to improve my coding skills and learn new techniques.

My professional values are centered around showing quality work, being a reliable team player, and striving for continuous improvement. I am motivated by the challenges and opportunities in the tech industry.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, reading books, drawing and cycling. These activities help me maintain a balanced life and stay inspired.

Looking ahead, I aim to become a full fledged video game developer in the future. For now I'm enhancing my skills in IT so I can enhance my resume and broaden my experience.

If you would like to connect or collaborate, feel free to reach out to me through my contact section below. I'm always open to new opportunities and connections!
