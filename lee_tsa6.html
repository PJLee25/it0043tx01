Amiya

Rhodes Island

The public face of Rhodes Island and its top executive. Although Amiya appears to be a young and inexperienced girl, she is universally trusted and highly qualified for her position. Amiya now leads the island in fighting for the future of the Infected and striving to exorcise the dark shadow of Originium from the land.

Shiroko

Abydos High School

The sports-loving field captain of the Countermeasures Committee of the Abydos High School. Shiroko is a girl of few words and shows little emotions alike. She may seem aloof with her appearance, but deeply cares about Abydos High School the most.

Enterprise

Eagle Union

Enterprise, also known as USS Enterprise, is the poster girl and one of the main protagonists of the Azur Lane video game along with the Commander, the main protagonists of the namesake anime, and a part of the Yorktown-class sisters (alongside Yorktown and Hornet). She is an Aircraft Carrier-type KAN-SEN supersoldier of the Eagle Union.

M4A1

AR Team

M4A1 is often described as a rather unreliable T-Doll lacking in decisiveness. Despite this, AR Team, while not as capable as other elite T-Dolls teams, enjoys a much greater autonomy and is at its best under M4A1's command. According to M16A1, M4A1's indecision is due to her being “too smart” because of the independence granted by her special Neural Cloud.

Kiana Kaslana

St. Freya Academy

Kiana Kaslana is a silver-haired Valkyrie of House Kaslana and the main protagonist of Honkai Impact 3rd's Part 1: The Moon's Origin and Finality. She is a genetic clone of the original Kiana, Bianka Ataegina, and the Herrscher of the Void, Sirin. Her mind is that of Sirin, making her essentially a reincarnation of Sirin.
